\section{Data Filtering}
\label{sec:data-filtering}
We label each supervised (SFT), preference, and RLVR sample with the minimum calendar year consistent with its question--answer bundle.
The prompt structure is detailed in \Cref{sec:sft-prompt}.
We considered deterministic filtering, but it was difficult to capture all edge cases with a rule-based approach.
The latest sweep (session \texttt{\FilteringSession}) processed \SFTProcessedCount{} SFT examples, \PreferenceProcessedCount{} preference examples, and three RLVR datasets (\RlvrsGsmCount{} GSM, \RlvrsMathCount{} MATH, \RlvrsIfevalCount{} IFEval) with a conservative policy that uses the most recent referenced year.
\Cref{fig:sft,fig:dpo,fig:rlvr} show year and category distributions for each dataset family and validate cutoff integrity.
